"Part B: Etiquette Checklist

* Do’s

1.Think before posting anything online, as it creates your digital image.

2.Keep your profiles professional, especially on platforms used for academics or careers.

3.Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information.

4.Share useful, positive, and meaningful content.

5.Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely in comments or messages.

* Don’ts

1.Do not share personal information like phone number, address, or passwords publicly.

2.Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.

3.Do not spread fake news or unverified information.

4.Avoid using weak passwords or the same password across multiple accounts.

5.Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying."
