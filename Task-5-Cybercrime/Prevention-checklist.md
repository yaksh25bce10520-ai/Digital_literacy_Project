"Part B: Stay Safe Online (Prevention Checklist)

General Safety Tips

• Always check the sender before accepting any UPI payment request. If something feels suspicious, do not proceed.

• Remember that entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it.

• Avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading files from untrusted sources.

• Keep your banking apps and mobile software updated to avoid security risks.

• Use strong and unique passwords for your apps and do not share them with anyone.

• Be careful while using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions, as it may not be secure.

UPI & Financial Safety Tips

• Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.

• Do not accept payment requests from unknown people, especially when selling items online.

• Always verify the payment status in your app instead of trusting screenshots sent by others.

• Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown sources, as they can lead to unauthorized payments.

Reporting & Awareness

• In case of any fraud, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in).

• You can also call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for quick assistance."
